International Olympiads

IMO

International Mathematical Olympiad — The most prestigious mathematics competition worldwide.

🎯 Eligibility: Students under 20, not enrolled in university. Must qualify through national rounds (INMO for India).

🏆 Benefits: Global recognition, direct pathways to top universities (MIT, Cambridge, IITs), medalists often receive direct admission offers.

📝 How to Participate: Clear IOQM → INMO → IMO Training Camp → Represent India.

Apply → imo-official.org

IPhO

International Physics Olympiad — Elite physics competition testing theoretical and experimental skills.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through NSEP → INPhO → Training Camp.

🏆 Benefits: Opens doors to CERN, Max Planck, top IITs, international research networking.

📝 How to Participate: Register for NSEP via IAPT → Clear INPhO → Selected for camp.

Apply → ipho-new.org

IChO

International Chemistry Olympiad — Premier chemistry competition combining theory and lab work.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through NSEC → INChO → Training Camp.

🏆 Benefits: Excellent for chemistry research careers, strong profile for science scholarships.

📝 How to Participate: Register for NSEC via IAPT → Clear INChO → Training camp.

Apply → ichosc.org

IBO

International Biology Olympiad — Top biology competition with theoretical and practical rounds.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through NSEB → INBO → Training Camp.

🏆 Benefits: Exceptional for medical/biotech careers, research exposure, ideal for medicine aspirants.

📝 How to Participate: Register for NSEB via IAPT → Clear INBO → Training camp.

Apply → ibo-info.org

IOI

International Olympiad in Informatics — Elite programming and algorithms competition.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through ZCO/ZIO → INOI → Training Camp.

🏆 Benefits: Direct path to top CS programs, Google/Microsoft actively recruit medalists.

📝 How to Participate: Register for ZCO/ZIO via IARCS → Clear INOI → Training camp.

Apply → ioinformatics.org

IOAA

International Olympiad on Astronomy & Astrophysics — Combines physics and astronomy challenges.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through NSEA → INOA → Training Camp.

🏆 Benefits: Unique interdisciplinary profile, connects with astronomy research groups, excellent for astrophysics careers.

📝 How to Participate: Register for NSEA via IAPT → Clear INOA → Training camp.

Apply → ioaaastronomy.org

IESO

International Earth Science Olympiad — Earth systems, geology, environmental science.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 20, not enrolled in university. Qualify through national rounds.

🏆 Benefits: Perfect for environmental sciences, field-based research exposure, great for climate science research.

📝 How to Participate: Check national organizing body (usually geological societies).

Apply → itace.org

IJSO

International Junior Science Olympiad — Multidisciplinary science for younger students.

🎯 Eligibility: Under 15 years old as of 31st December of competition year.

🏆 Benefits: Early research exposure, builds foundational scientific thinking, perfect for starting research journey early.

📝 How to Participate: Register through national coordinator (HBCSE for India).

Apply → ijsoweb.org

National Olympiads (India)

INMO / IOQM

Indian National Mathematical Olympiad — Gateway to IMO team selection.

🎯 Eligibility: Indian students under 20, not enrolled in university.

🏆 Benefits: HBCSE recognition, intensive training camps, career in mathematical research, direct entry to ISI, CMI, IITs.

📝 How to Participate: Register for IOQM via MTAI → Clear → INMO → Training camp.

Apply → mtai.org.in

NSEP / NSEC / NSEB / NSEA

National Standard Examinations (IAPT) — Stepping stones for International Olympiads.

🎯 Eligibility: Indian students in Class 12 or below.

🏆 Benefits: Exposure to national-level competition, merit certificates, selection for advanced training camps.

📝 How to Participate: Register online via IAPT website before August each year.

Apply → iapt.org.in

ZCO / ZIO (IARCS)

Indian Computing Olympiad — Algorithmic problem-solving competition.

🎯 Eligibility: Indian students in Class 12 or below.

🏆 Benefits: Builds programming and logic skills, selection for IOI training camp, recognized in tech industry.

📝 How to Participate: Register online via IARCS website. ZCO is online, ZIO is postal.

Apply → iarcs.org.in

KVPY

Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana — Prestigious fellowship for science students.

🎯 Eligibility: Indian students in Class 11, 12, or 1st year of undergraduate (B.Sc./B.Stat./B.Math.).

🏆 Benefits: Direct financial support (₹5,000-₹7,000/month), IISc/IISER admission pathways, research internship opportunities.

📝 How to Participate: Register online via KVPY IISc portal. Exam held annually in November.

Apply → kvpy.iisc.ac.in

HBCSE Olympiads

HBCSE Science Olympiads (Stage 2+) — Advanced selection stages for international olympiads.

🎯 Eligibility: Students who clear NSEs (NSEP/NSEC/NSEB/NSEA).

🏆 Benefits: Top-tier training, international representation, research mentorship.

📝 How to Participate: Automatically eligible after clearing Stage 1 NSE exams.

Apply → olympiads.hbcse.tifr.res.in

USA & Other Notable Olympiads

USAMO / AMC

USA Mathematical Olympiad / AMC — Premier American mathematics competitions.

🎯 Eligibility: Any student (international participation possible through AMC).

🏆 Benefits: International exposure, strong profile for US universities, advanced problem-solving skills.

📝 How to Participate: Register through AMC coordinator at your school or international testing centers.

Apply → maa.org

USAPhO / USNCO / USABO

USA Physics/Chemistry/Biology Olympiads — High-level competitions.

🎯 Eligibility: US citizens/residents typically, but study materials available globally.

🏆 Benefits: Rigorous training material, great for college applications, research experience development.

📝 How to Participate: For US students: register via AAPT/ACS. International students can use past papers for practice.

Physics → aapt.org

BPhO / APhO / EuPhO

British/Asian/European Physics Olympiads — Diverse international competitions.

🎯 Eligibility: Varies by region. BPhO open to UK & international, APhO for Asian countries, EuPhO for European.

🏆 Benefits: Exposure to different problem styles, globally recognized, expands academic network.

📝 How to Participate: Register through respective national organizing bodies.

BPhO → bpho.org.uk

USACO

USA Computing Olympiad — Advanced programming competition.

🎯 Eligibility: Any student worldwide (online participation allowed).

🏆 Benefits: Ideal for CS aspirants, tech internships, algorithmic mastery for research. Highly respected by Silicon Valley.

📝 How to Participate: Create free account on USACO website → participate in monthly contests.

Apply → usaco.org

🏆 Benefits: Deep subject focus, international recognition, research-oriented problems.

📝 How to Participate: Register through official website (OCS Olympiad).

IChC

International Chemistry Competition — Global chemistry challenge.

🏆 Benefits: Research-focused problems, international participation, certificate of excellence.
